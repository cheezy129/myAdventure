THE EIFFLE TOWER WAS BUILT BETWEEN 1887 & 1889







ENGINEERING SHOWCASE
The tower was designed by Gustave Eiffel, a renowned engineer, and his team. It served as a remarkable engineering feat and a testament to France's prowess in the field. The construction of the tower demonstrated the possibilities of using iron as a structural material on a large scale.
The eiffle tower serves several purposes:
I. Landmark Symbol
II. Tourist attraction
III. Communicating and broadcasting
